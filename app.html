FAQ

What is The Orbit? +
A 21-day guided protocol that uses breath, sound, and vibration to restore your nervous system to coherence. Three weeks. Three planetary phases. Each session is 20 minutes, sequenced through arrival, immersion, and integration.
Do I need the Quantasana pod? +
No. The digital protocol works with headphones alone. The pod amplifies the experience with vibroacoustic vibration — but the breath and sound protocols are effective on their own.
How long are the daily sessions? +
20 minutes. Each session moves through three phases: arrival, immersion, and integration. The timing is calibrated to your nervous system's response curve.
What techniques are used? +
Resonance breathing at 6 breaths per minute, binaural frequency entrainment at 40Hz gamma and 4-8Hz theta, vibroacoustic therapy, and guided sensory grounding. All backed by peer-reviewed research.
What results can I expect? +
Clinical protocols using these modalities show measurable improvements in HRV, cortisol reduction, and brainwave coherence within 2-3 weeks of consistent practice. Your app tracks these shifts in real time.
Do I need prior meditation experience? +
No. The protocol is designed to meet you where you are. The breath cues, sound frequencies, and vibration do the work — you just show up.
Can I restart or repeat the program? +
Yes. Many practitioners cycle through The Orbit multiple times. Each pass deepens the practice.
Is there an app? +
Yes. The Quantasana app orchestrates every session — guiding your breath, adjusting frequencies, and tracking your HRV. Available on web, with iOS coming soon.
