The Science

Built on real physiology, not wellness vibe.

Every design decision in the QUANTASANA protocol is informed by established research in HRV biofeedback, vibroacoustic therapy, and gamma brainwave entrainment.

6

Breaths per minute

The resonance frequency for maximum heart rate variability. At ~0.1 Hz, baroreflex sensitivity peaks and the autonomic nervous system achieves coherence between cardiac, respiratory, and vascular rhythms.

Lehrer et al., Applied Psychophysiology & Biofeedback

40 Hz

Gamma entrainment frequency

Non-invasive 40 Hz sensory stimulation activates microglia to clear amyloid plaques, enhances gamma oscillation power, and improves cognitive function across multiple cortical regions.

Tsai Lab, MIT Picower Institute · Nature, Cell, Neuron

76%

Reduction in cognitive decline

In the Phase 2 OVERTURE clinical trial, daily 40 Hz multi-sensory stimulation showed a 76% reduction in cognitive decline compared to sham control over 6 months in mild-to-moderate Alzheimer's patients.

OVERTURE Trial · Frontiers in Neurology 2024

30–120 Hz

Vibroacoustic therapy range

Pacinian corpuscles and mechanoreceptors respond optimally in the 30–120 Hz range. Whole-body vibration at these frequencies reduces cortisol, increases blood flow, and activates deep tissue parasympathetic pathways.

Skille (1989) · Sensors 2024 · FDA Class I 21 CFR 890.5975
