About QUANTASANA

QUANTASANA is a wellness technology company based in Plano, Texas, building the first integrated system that delivers guided breathwork, precision sound frequencies, vibroacoustic vibration, and 40 Hz gamma light entrainment through a single handcrafted lounge chair and companion app.


Founded in 2026, the company designs every session around a three-phase neurological protocol (Arrival → Immersion → Integration) informed by published research in HRV biofeedback, vibroacoustic therapy, and gamma brainwave entrainment from institutions including MIT's Picower Institute.


The Founders Edition chair — handcrafted in walnut europly with four independent vibration zones — is now accepting reservations. An in-person demo studio operates in Plano, TX by appointment.
